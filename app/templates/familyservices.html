Child and Spousal Support(Alimony)

Child Support

Whether you face a child support liability or need extra financial help taking care of your children, we will walk you through the impact and possibilities of a child support order. The often complicated tables that the Court may provide can be confusing and lack direction. We aim to break down the complicated into simple solutions for you.

Spousal Support

Regardless of whether you had a long or short marriage, there is the very real possibility that spousal support can and will be ordered in your case. Rarely do two spouses earn identical incomes. We will walk you through obtaining additional financial help for yourself, or help limit the liabilities that you face in defense of a spousal support request.

Domestic Violence Restraining Order

If you feel you may need protection or if you have been accused, get help from us

A large number of family law cases do involve domestic violence issues. This common issue has been repeatedly tried at Lun & Associates. If there is a domestic violence issue that you believe may exist in your case, we will walk you through making sure you are adequately protected with you are the victim or the accused. It is an extremely important part of your family law case which will can have major consequences with your child custody and general support issues.

Modification of Previous Orders

Family Law almost always allows for modification of a current order

Family law is unique in that a judges order is often not the final say on an issue. Over time things may change, necessitating a change in a particular order. If you feel that a particular order, whether through a judge or agreement, is no longer fitting to your current life situation, we can help you decide what next move is best for you.
